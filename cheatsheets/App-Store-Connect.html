```html App Store Connect Cheat Sheet

App Store Connect Cheat Sheet

Commands

  • asc app list - Lists all apps
  • asc app modify - Modifies an app
  • asc app create - Creates a new app
  • asc app delete - Deletes an app

Syntax

  • asc [command] [subcommand] [parameters]
  • asc app list --limit 100 - Lists first 100 apps
  • asc app modify --app [id] --name [new-name]

Best Practices

  • Always update App Store Connect regularly
  • Use version control to manage your app
  • Always test your app before deployment
```
